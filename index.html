I build end-to-end machine learning systems grounded in rigorous experimental methodology — spanning supervised learning, model reliability, production MLOps, and reproducible research workflows.

My work is centered on making AI trustworthy by design: robust to distribution shift, transparent in its reasoning, and auditable across its full lifecycle — from data ingestion to deployment.

As a Ph.D. candidate at Chonnam National University, I am actively expanding into deep learning — integrating neural architectures into the reliable, production-oriented systems I have built with classical ML. Targeting postdoctoral research at Korea's premier S&T institutes through the InnoCORE program.
