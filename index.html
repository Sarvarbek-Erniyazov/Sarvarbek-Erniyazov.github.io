PhD candidate at Chonnam National University, AI Lab, advised by Prof. Chang Gyoon Lim. My research spans Graph Neural Networks, Federated Learning, and Mixture-of-Experts architectures, with applied work in energy forecasting and smart grid systems.

Beyond domain-specific applications, I am committed to building trustworthy and reproducible ML systems — robust to distribution shift, transparent in reasoning, and auditable across the full lifecycle from data ingestion to deployment.

I am actively targeting the InnoCORE 2026 postdoctoral fellowship to contribute to Korea's premier AI+S&T research initiatives.
