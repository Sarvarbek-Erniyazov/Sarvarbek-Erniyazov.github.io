PhD candidate at Chonnam National University, AI Lab, advised by Prof. Chang Gyoon Lim. My research investigates Graph Neural Networks, Federated Learning, Mixture-of-Experts architectures, and Language Models for real-world intelligent systems.

Through four national R&D grants (NRF, TIPA, INNOPOLIS), I have contributed to applied research in energy forecasting, virtual power plants, satellite-ground data integration, and AI-based anomaly detection. I am actively expanding into Physical AI, multimodal vision systems, and safety-critical AI applications.
